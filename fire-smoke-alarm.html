🔥 Fire and Smoke Alarm System

Objective

To detect smoke and fire at an early stage and alert people using a buzzer.

Components Required

  • MQ-2 Smoke Sensor
  • Arduino Uno
  • Buzzer
  • LED
  • Jumper Wires
  • Breadboard

Working Principle

The MQ-2 sensor continuously monitors smoke concentration. When smoke exceeds a preset threshold, the Arduino activates the buzzer and LED to provide an alert.

Applications

  • Homes
  • Offices
  • Factories
  • Warehouses

Future Scope

Can be integrated with IoT to send alerts to mobile phones.
