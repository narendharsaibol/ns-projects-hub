💧 Water Tank Level Indicator

Abstract

The Water Tank Level Indicator monitors water levels in storage tanks and helps prevent overflow and water wastage.

Components Required

  • water level Sensor
  • Arduino Uno
  • Buzzer
  • LED
  • Breadboard
  • Jumper Wires

Working Principle

The sensor continuously monitors the water level and indicates different levels using LEDs. A buzzer alerts when the tank becomes full.

Applications

  • Homes
  • Apartments
  • Industries
  • Agricultural Water Storage

Future Scope

Can be connected to an automatic motor controller for complete automation.
