⚡ Smart Energy Meter

Abstract

Smart Energy Meter measures electrical energy consumption and displays usage information in real time. It helps users monitor and manage power usage efficiently.

Components Required

  • Arduino Uno
  • Current Sensor
  • Voltage Sensor
  • LCD Display
  • Power Supply
  • Jumper Wires

Working Principle

The sensors measure voltage and current. The Arduino calculates power consumption and displays the readings on the LCD screen.

Applications

  • Home Energy Monitoring
  • Industrial Power Monitoring
  • Smart Grid Systems
