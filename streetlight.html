💡 Smart Street Light

Abstract

Smart Street Light is an automatic lighting system that turns street lights ON during the night and OFF during the daytime using an LDR sensor. This helps reduce electricity consumption and improves energy efficiency.

Components Required

  • Arduino Uno
  • LDR Sensor
  • LED
  • 220Ω Resistor
  • Breadboard
  • Jumper Wires

Working Principle

The LDR continuously monitors the surrounding light intensity. When darkness is detected, the Arduino turns ON the LED street light. During daylight, the light is switched OFF automatically.

Applications

  • Smart Cities
  • Automatic Road Lighting
  • Energy Saving Systems
  • Public Infrastructure Projects
