⚡ Gas Leakage Detector

Abstract

Gas Leakage Detector is a safety system designed to detect LPG gas leakage using an MQ-2 gas sensor. When gas concentration exceeds a predefined level, a buzzer and LED alert the user to prevent accidents.

Components Required

  • Arduino Uno
  • MQ-2 Gas Sensor
  • Buzzer
  • LED
  • 220Ω Resistor
  • Jumper Wires
  • Breadboard

Working Principle

The MQ-2 sensor continuously monitors gas concentration in the environment. If gas leakage is detected, the Arduino activates a buzzer and LED warning system.
