🧴 Automatic Hand Sanitizer Dispenser

Abstract

The Automatic Hand Sanitizer Dispenser dispenses sanitizer without physical contact, improving hygiene and reducing the spread of germs.

Components Required

  • IR Sensor
  • Arduino Uno
  • Breadboard
  • Jumper Wires
  • Mini water pump
  • Sanitizer Container
  • Battery

Working Principle

When a hand is detected near the sensor, the Arduino activates the pump and dispenses sanitizer automatically.

Applications

  • School
  • Hospitals
  • Offices
  • public places

Future Scope

Can include automatic sanitizer level monitoring and IoT notifications
