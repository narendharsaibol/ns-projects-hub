🎓 CGPA Calculator App

Abstract

CGPA Calculator App helps students calculate their Semester GPA (SGPA) and overall CGPA quickly and accurately. The app simplifies academic performance tracking and reduces manual calculations.

Technologies Used

  • Android studio
  • Kotlin (or Java)/li>
  • XML
  • SQLite Database
  • Material Design

Working Principle

The user enters subject grades or semester SGPA values. The application processes the data using the CGPA formula and instantly displays the CGPA and percentage.

Applications

  • Diploma Students
  • Engineering Students
  • Colleges
  • Academic Performance Tracking
  • Educational Institutions
