LEGO – AI Pet Companion

Abstract

LEGO is an AI-powered pet companion built using ESP32, OLED display, microphone, speaker, and touch interaction. It can communicate with users, display emotions, respond to voice commands, and provide an interactive companion experience.

Components Required

  • ESP32 Development Board
  • OLED Display
  • INMP441 Microphone
  • MAX98357A Amplifier
  • Speaker
  • Push Button
  • Touch Sensor (GPIO 15)
  • Jumper Wires & Power Supply

Working Principle

The microphone captures the user's voice and sends it to the ESP32 for processing. The AI system generates responses, which are displayed on the OLED screen and played through the speaker. Touch and button inputs allow users to interact with LEGO like a digital pet.

Applications

  • Personal AI Companion
  • Educational Assistant
  • Smart Home Interaction
  • IoT Learning Project
  • Tech Fest Demonstrations

Future Scope

LEGO can be enhanced with facial recognition, memory-based conversations, mobile app connectivity, home automation features, and multilingual voice support.
