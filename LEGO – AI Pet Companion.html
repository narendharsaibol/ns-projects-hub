LEGO – AI Pet Companion

Abstract

The Smart Irrigation System automatically waters plants based on soil moisture levels, conserving water and improving crop growth.

Components Required

  • Soil Moisture Sensor
  • Arduino Uno
  • Water pump
  • Relay

Working Principle

The soil moisture sensor measures moisture levels. When soil becomes dry, the Arduino switches ON the pump automatically.

Applications

  • Agriculture
  • Gardens
  • Greenhouses
  • Smart Farming

Future Scope

Can be integrated with weather forecasting and mobile monitoring systems.
