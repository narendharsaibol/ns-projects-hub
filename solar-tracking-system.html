☀️ Solar Tracking System

Abstract

Solar Tracking System automatically follows the position of the sun to maximize the efficiency of solar panels and increase power generation.

Components Required

  • Arduino Uno
  • LDR Sensors
  • Servo Motor
  • Solar Panel
  • Jumper Wires

Working Principle

The LDR sensors detect the direction of sunlight. The Arduino controls the servo motor to rotate the solar panel towards the brightest light source.

Applications

  • Solar Power Plants
  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Energy Optimization Projects
