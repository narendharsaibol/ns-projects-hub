🔋 Human Body Power Generator

Abstract

Human Body Power Generator generates electrical energy from human movement using piezoelectric sensors. The generated energy can be used for low-power devices.

Components Required

  • Piezoelectric Sensors
  • LED
  • Capacitor
  • Arduino Uno (Optional)
  • Connecting Wires

Working Principle

When pressure or movement is applied to the piezoelectric sensor, it generates electrical voltage. This energy can be stored and used for small electronic applications.

Applications

  • Wearable Devices
  • Energy Harvesting Systems
  • Smart Footpaths
  • Emergency Power Sources
